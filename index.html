About us :

Sähkö Recordings is a Finnish independent record label, founded in Turku and based in Helsinki, Finland. Sähkö ("electricity" in Finnish) was founded by Tommi Grönlund in 1993. The label gained international acclaim by its minimalist electronic releases. Besides Sähkö, Sähkö Recordings releases music under such sublabels as Puu, Keys of Life and Jazzpuu. Sähkö and its sublabels have released records among all such Finnish artists as Ø (a.k.a. Mika Vainio), Pan sonic, Jimi Tenor and Freestyle Man (a.k.a. Sasse Lindblad) and with internationally acclaimed artists such as Ryoji Ikeda and Carl Michael von Hausswolff.
